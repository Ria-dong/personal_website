Website Design: Fleurs Fiction Commission

Fleurs Fiction Commission is a self-built website for a virtual writing commission, aim to let writers and funders connect with each other. It contains a simple searching function, a gallery, some self-made animation, self-written novels and an alternative css to adapt to mobile devices.

It is not a student design project that came from nowhere, it is based on the actual difficulties of the lack of channels and management of fan-fiction transaction between writers and funders, especially compare with drawing.
