Coles Supermarkets Australia Pty Ltd was founded in 1914 and has become a leading, trusted Australian retailer. The purpose of Coles is to sustainably feed all Australians to help them lead healthier, happier lives. Coles Home Delivery is a part of Coles Online service, aiming to satisfy customers who shop online rather than choose items in-store (Coles Annual Report 2022). There are 2 major patterns of receiving goods: Click & Collect and Home Delivery.
There are 3 patterns of home delivery depending on the distance and time. This article focus on the standard delivery system.
