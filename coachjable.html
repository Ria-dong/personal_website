Website Design: Fleurs Fiction Commission

This project is a data mining collaboration between ANU CBE and Practera. I hold the position of a data scientist in the project. My responsibilities include conducting preliminary research for the services providers (business coaches) that startup company X is looking for, as well as providing advice on recruiting and attracting professionals for its future.

What you are currently seeing is an interactive dashboard, displaying a brief summary, user profiles, and recommended industry focuses. They present the overall characteristics of the service providers, various types of specialties, and the industries and psychological needs of their sources (and demands).

In my investigation, I found that these coaches have distinctive work experience, educational levels, and part-time work. As business coaches, they often hold relevant management positions within companies (even at the C-level), enabling them to keep up with developments. They are not primarily motivated by money when coaching beginners; many even do it as volunteers. This has reinforced our belief that in the initial stages, the company can establish a strong community atmosphere to quickly attract more aspiring managers who are eager to teach.
